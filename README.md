>Ardas Sculks (1.21.1+) - Source Code & Assets

**Arda's Sculks** allows you to craft more powerful tools and items by enhancing the sculk mechanics in the game. Explore new dimensions, fight unique bosses, and discover the secrets of the Ancient World.

> **IMPORTANT:** Versions 1.4 and above require **GECKOLIB**.
> If Geckolib is not installed, the game will crash!

---

## Dimensions & Portals

### The Ancient World
A new dimension filled with mystery.
* **New Portal:** A new structure added to the game.
* **Igniter:** Craft the **Ancient World Portal Igniter** using materials collected from the Ancient City to access this dimension.

---

## Biomes

Explore unique and dangerous environments:

* **Soul Sculk Plains:** The grass here slows creatures down. Soul sensors hidden around can slow players down even further.
* **Sculk Plains:** Flat, vast lands in the new dimension.
* **Black Birch Forest:** A dangerous biome with dense trees where the *Creaking* creature lives.
* **Sculk Deserts:** Arid areas with no trees. Look for ancient portals here to return home.
* **Wisterya Forest:** A beautiful biome surrounded by rocks, featuring colorful trees.
* **Sculk Forest:** Similar to Sculk Plains but with more trees and fewer bones.
* **Radioactive Balsa Forest:** A toxic and mysterious forest.

---

## Bosses & Mobs

### Bosses
* **Sculk Golem:** Found in the new boss area. Drops the **Sculk Crystal** needed to craft the *Sculkerite Multi-Tool*.
* **Sculk Ghost:** A flying enemy that hunts players.

### New Creatures
* **Sculk Creeper:** Explodes creating darkness and a slowing effect.
* **Sculk Skeleton:** Its arrows push you back and apply darkness.
* **Sculk Enderman:** A sculk-themed variant of the Enderman.
* **Sculk Fox & Fish:** Sculk-themed wildlife.
* **Agile Slime:** Very fast! Splits in two when killed.
* **Sculk Creaking:** An enemy with a powerful arm.
* **Shadow Hunter:** A dangerous hunter lurking in the dark.
* **Radioactive Warden:** A more deadly version of the Warden.
* **Sculk Villager:** A merchant selling items specific to the Ancient City and Deep Dark.

---

## Items & Equipment

* **Sculk Arms:** Gives you fists as powerful as the Warden's! Crafted with materials from the Ancient City.
* **Sculkerite Items:** A new tier of equipment **stronger than Netherite**.
* **Upgrade Template:** Found in Ancient City chests.
* **Sculk Flowers:** New decorative plants and light sources.
* **Wood Sets:** Full wood sets for Sculk, Black Birch, Wisterya, and Radioactive Balsa trees.

---

## Mechanics: Advanced Furnace

Take your Netherite items to the next level!

**How to use:**
1.  **Slots 1 & 2:** Add a Molten Iron Bucket and a Molten Sculk Bucket.
2.  **Slot 3:** Place the item you want to transform.
3.  **Fuel:** Add fuel below the 3rd slot.
4.  **Result:** Collect your transformed item and the empty buckets.
